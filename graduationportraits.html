How do I book with you?

If you're interested in setting up a session, visit my contact form and send me an email! We'll set up a short 10-minute call to:

  • Get to know each other
  • Schedule your session date
  • Plan locations and outfits
  • Discuss any special preferences

A 25% deposit is required to secure your booking, with the remainder due on session day.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my session?

ASAP! As grad season rolls around, it can be difficult to get all the settings you want. Due to lighting constraints, I only have two slots available per day (golden hour mornings and evenings).

Can I bring family/friends/pets?

Of course! There's no extra fee to have your family or furry friends join in on your photos. For larger groups (3+ people), consider the Premium package for optimal timing.

How many photos do you deliver?

I believe that getting you more is better! My albums typically consist of 80-140 edited selects per hour (with more edited photos delivered when you add on more graduates to a session). You'll receive a private online gallery to download all high-res images.

What if I'm feeling nervous?

No worries! I'm all about making your session fun and relaxed. I'll provide a variety of prompts and poses and guide you throughout your whole session. Most clients feel comfortable within the first 10 minutes!
