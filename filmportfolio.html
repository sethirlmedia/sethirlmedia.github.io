My approach to Filmmaking

With a strong background in cinematography and storytelling, I've worked across narrative, commerical, and creative film, often taking on multiple roles including director, editor, and cinematographer. My focus follows the convergence of music and film, and how sound and score can shape emotion and influence storytelling. I aim to create visually striking work where music plays a central, expressive role.
